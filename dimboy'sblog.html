My History

Posted on Tuesday, 12th August, 2025

It all started when my parents told me I couldn't spend my whole vacation on video games and enlightened me about coding and also said I might be able earn money. At first I wasn't interested but then I decided to give it a shot because:

  1. I wanted to earn some money
  2. I didn't have a choice
  3. I didn't have any other thing to do
  4. I wanted to make my parents proud

It sounded fun at first and it was.

Why I chose Khan Academy

Posted on Tuesday, 12th August, 2025

I actually used Khan Academy before now when I was younger, but I stopped due to the lack of interest and started again years later and forgot how fun it was. It started with a simple google search. I mostly chose Khan Academy because it's a free open source for curious kids like me in search for skills like making websites with code, it turned out to be easy when I started with drawing with code and gradually advanced to HTML and CSS. Overall, I don't think there is any other website I would've chosen other than this.

What I have learned

Posted on Tuesday, 12th August, 2025

I have learned a lot of things on Khan Academy.

The first thing I learned was how to make drawings with code. I started with codes like: ellipse(Circle), rect(Square), line(Line), fill, stroke, strokeweight, etc. Then I advanced to HTML which is the foundation of webpages and learned that it's mostly all about different tags for different functions, then I learned CSS for styling webpages.

What I have made

I have conquered many challenges and perfected many projects on my journey in Khan Academy but was all worth it to feel like I've spent my vacation wisely. I have made stuff like: drawings, webpages, links to other webpages, customized my own blog, created my own recipe book, my own travel website and many more. I am currently on Unit 2 Lesson 4 and will go on and keep growing.

Conclusion

In conclusion, I would like to thank Pamela for the very helpful and interactive lessons. I recommend Khan Academy because of the fun and user-friendly lessons and assignments, it has really helped me.
